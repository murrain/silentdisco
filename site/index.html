Troubleshooting
  • Join the event Wi-Fi (same network as dj.dance).
  • If audio crackles, move closer to the DJ Wi-Fi or try again.
  • Browsers cannot play UDP — use an app (VLC, nPlayer, MX Player, mpv, etc.).
